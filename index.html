My interests sit at the intersection of real-time graphics, computer vision, and AI integration.

Most recently I led Virtual Driving School, a webcam motion-controlled driving simulator that placed 2nd of 30 teams at the Giiku CAMP 2025 hackathon in Tokyo. I like shipping a real artifact, pushing it for performance, and wiring in AI where it genuinely helps.
